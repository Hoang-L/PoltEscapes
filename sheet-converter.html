🗝️ Sheet → data.js

Paste your Google Sheet data and convert it to escape room entries

How to use
1

In Google Sheets, select all your data including the header row, then Copy (Ctrl+C / Cmd+C)

2

Paste into the Paste Sheet Data box below — it'll auto-detect your columns

3

Map your columns to the right fields, fill in any defaults, then hit Convert

4

Copy the output and paste it into js/data.js inside your ROOMS array
